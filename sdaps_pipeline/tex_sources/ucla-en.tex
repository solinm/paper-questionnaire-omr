\section{Scale}
When answering the questions, you could take account of the following: 
\begin{itemize}
    \item There are no right or wrong answers
    \item We would like you to be completely honest
    \item In answering the questions it is best to think of your life as it generally is now \\(we all have some good or bad days)
\end{itemize}
